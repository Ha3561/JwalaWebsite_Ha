Cultural Committee

At Jwalamukhi Hostel, we take great pride in our cultural heritage and the vibrant community that thrives within our walls. The Jwalamukhi Hostel Cultural Committee is here to ensure that our rich culture remains at the forefront of our co-curricular activities, inspiring and uniting all house members in their pursuit of excellence.


As the torchbearers of our cultural heritage, our committee takes immense pride in organizing the highly anticipated cultural fest of Jwalamukhi known as "Mahsheed." This grand celebration brings together talents from within our hostel and beyond. Our committee's mission is to ensure that our traditions, values, and artistic expressions thrive in our co-curricular activities. We take pride in fostering a sense of unity and belonging among all residents as we actively participate and encourage the house residents to participate in competitions and events held within and outside our institute. With great enthusiasm, we organize and conduct a diverse range of events throughout the year. From captivating cultural festivals to awe-inspiring talent showcases, our committee endeavors to create memorable experiences that resonate with each and every one of you.


As residents of Jwalamukhi Hostel, you are at the heart of our cultural endeavors. We invite you to immerse yourselves in the array of co-curricular activities taking place in our institute. Whether you seek to showcase your talents, expand your artistic horizons, or simply wish to witness the magic of cultural expression, the Jwalamukhi Hostel Cultural Committee is here for you. If you have any questions or would like to know more about the exciting co-curricular activities happening within our institute, feel free to reach out to any of our dedicated committee members. We are more than happy to provide you with all the information you need.


Let us come together, embrace our cultural spirit, and make Jwalamukhi Hostel a place where cultural traditions flourish, and lifelong memories are created.
